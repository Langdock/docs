### Overview

At Langdock, security is our top priority. We understand the importance of your data and are committed to safeguarding it. Our ChatGPT for Teams application is designed with a strong emphasis on compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other European regulations.

### Hosting

All Langdock services are hosted within the European Union, ensuring that data remains within the jurisdiction of European data protection laws.

### Certifications

Langdock is actively pursuing various internationally recognized certifications, including:

- **SOC 2:** This certification is widely recognized as a gold standard for data security. The SOC 2 certification involves a thorough audit of our data handling practices, ensuring that we maintain high standards of security, availability, processing integrity, confidentiality, and privacy.

- **ISO 27001:** This is a globally recognized standard for the establishment, maintenance, and certification of an information security management system (ISMS). The certification confirms that Langdock has implemented the security practices outlined in this standard.

- **GDPR:** We are committed to complying with GDPR, which is designed to harmonize data privacy laws across Europe, protect and empower EU citizens' data privacy, and reshape the way organizations approach data privacy.

These certifications are expected to be completed in the early Autumn of 2023, further strengthening our commitment to data security.

### Data Protection

Langdock is committed to protecting your data. Our security measures include encryption, access controls, secure network design, and regular audits. We also provide tools that allow you to control and manage your data.

### Compliance

Langdock is committed to maintaining compliance with GDPR and other European regulations. This includes ensuring the rights of data subjects, such as the right to access, correct, delete, and transfer personal data.

### Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about security at Langdock, please contact our Data Protection Officer at security@langdock.com.

Please note that this information is subject to change as we continue to improve our security practices and achieve additional certifications. Be sure to check back regularly for the most up-to-date information.
